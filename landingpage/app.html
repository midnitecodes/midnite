The App

The Midnite app is the social pit lane for drivers who want more than a feed. It helps you find trusted parts, log real work on your builds, host and attend local meets, and connect with people who will actually help you finish the job.

Problem — You’re lost in noise

Scrolling endless posts and sellers with no trust — parts break, builds stall, and you waste time and money chasing bad advice.

Where you want to be

Know exactly where to buy trusted parts, document your progress, and meet people who can help you finish your build — quickly and safely.

How Midnite solves it

Verified community listings, garage logs to track maintenance and upgrades, event tools to organize meets, and social features that reward trusted contributors.

Marketplace & Trust

A private marketplace focused on seller reputation, escrow-friendly swaps, and community-verified listings so you’ll buy with confidence.

Available soon on App Store & Google Play — join the beta to claim your OG badge.

Feature details

Below are small examples of how each part of the app helps you get real work done, not just likes.

Feed & Stories

Showcase step-by-step builds with timestamps, parts lists, and annotations so others can reproduce or help. Save posts to private collections for future reference.

Garage Logs

Track every repair and upgrade — capture costs, odometer, photos, and receipts. Export logs for resale transparency or warranty claims.

Events & Meets

Create private or public meet pages with RSVP limits, safety notes, and in-app check-ins. Hold charity drives and offer reserved spots to OGs.

Marketplace

List parts with build history, seller reputation, and optional escrow handling. Buyers can filter by condition, location, and trusted sellers.
